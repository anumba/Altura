Enterprise Student Management Portal

A centralized platform that automated student record management, attendance tracking, and parent communication for a growing educational institution — reducing administrative workload while improving data accessibility across all stakeholders.

The Problem

The institution managed student records, attendance, and grade reporting through disconnected spreadsheets and manual processes. Administrative staff spent hours daily on routine data entry. Parents had no direct access to student progress, requiring phone calls for basic updates. As enrollment grew, the system became increasingly unsustainable.

The Approach

Detailed workflow analysis with administrators, teachers, and staff identified the highest-impact bottlenecks. The system was designed around role-based access for four user types: administrators, teachers, students, and parents. We automated the most time-consuming manual tasks first, then built additional features iteratively based on actual usage.

The Solution

A web-based student management system with automated attendance tracking, real-time grade calculation, and parent portal access. The platform handles complete enrollment workflows, generates report cards automatically, and provides administrative dashboards with key metrics. Teachers can input data from any device.

The Outcome

Administrative workload for routine tasks decreased 60%. Enrollment processing time dropped from days to hours. Report generation reduced from a multi-day manual process to under 10 minutes. System has been in continuous operation for over two years at 99.8% uptime. Parent engagement increased significantly with 24/7 data access.

Stack: React Node.js PostgreSQL AWS REST API
Digital Infrastructure Professional Services

Professional Services Corporate Website

Complete digital foundation for a consulting firm — including corporate website, business email infrastructure, and content management system — establishing reliable online presence with minimal ongoing technical overhead.

The Problem

The consulting firm had operated without a professional web presence for three years. Potential clients frequently requested a website to verify legitimacy before scheduling consultations. DIY solutions had been abandoned due to complexity and poor results. The firm needed a professional digital presence but lacked internal technical resources.

The Approach

Complete digital infrastructure was established from the ground up: domain registration, DNS configuration, business email with proper security protocols, and hosting environment. Website content structure was mapped to the client acquisition funnel. We implemented a CMS allowing the firm to update content without technical knowledge.

The Solution

A performance-optimized corporate website featuring service descriptions, consultant profiles, case study summaries, and contact forms. Business email with proper SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records. Automated SSL certificate management. CMS allowing staff to update content independently. Complete documentation for common tasks.

The Outcome

The firm now requires less than one hour per month of internal technical attention. Inbound consultation requests increased 40% within the first quarter after launch. Website loads in under two seconds on standard connections. The firm has independently updated service offerings and case studies multiple times. Technical support tickets average under one per month.

Stack: HTML/CSS/JS Strapi CMS Nginx CloudFlare CDN Let's Encrypt

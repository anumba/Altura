Custom Software Development

Enterprise Student Management Portal

A centralized platform that automated student record management, attendance tracking, and parent communication for a growing educational institution, reducing administrative workload while improving data accessibility.

The Problem

The institution was managing student records, attendance, and grade reporting through disconnected spreadsheets and manual processes. Administrative staff spent hours daily on routine data entry. Parents had no direct access to student progress information, requiring phone calls and emails for basic updates. As enrollment grew, the system became increasingly unsustainable, creating bottlenecks in enrollment processing and report generation.

The Approach

We conducted detailed workflow analysis with administrators, teachers, and support staff to understand existing processes and pain points. The system was designed around role-based access for four user types: administrators, teachers, students, and parents. We prioritized automating the most time-consuming manual tasks first, then built additional features iteratively based on actual usage patterns. The interface was structured to match how staff already thought about their work, minimizing the learning curve.

The Solution

A web-based student management system with automated attendance tracking, real-time grade calculation, and parent portal access. The platform handles complete enrollment workflows, generates report cards and transcripts automatically, and provides dashboards showing key metrics for administrators. Teachers can input grades and attendance from any device, and parents receive automatic notifications for important updates. All data is centralized with proper access controls and audit logging.

The Outcome

Administrative workload for routine tasks decreased by 60%, allowing staff to focus on educational quality rather than data management. Parent engagement increased significantly with 24/7 access to student information. Enrollment processing time dropped from days to hours. The institution now processes report generation in under 10 minutes compared to the previous multi-day manual process. System has been in continuous operation for over two years with 99.8% uptime.

Tech Stack Used

React Node.js PostgreSQL AWS REST API

Digital Infrastructure

Professional Services Corporate Website

Complete digital foundation for a consulting firm including corporate website, business email infrastructure, and content management system, establishing reliable online presence with minimal technical overhead.

The Problem

The consulting firm had been operating without a professional web presence for three years, relying entirely on referrals and LinkedIn outreach. Potential clients frequently requested a website to verify legitimacy before scheduling consultations. The firm had attempted DIY solutions with website builders but abandoned them due to complexity and poor results. They needed a professional digital presence but lacked internal technical resources or time to manage ongoing maintenance.

The Approach

We started by establishing complete digital infrastructure from the ground up: domain registration, DNS configuration, business email setup with proper security protocols, and hosting environment. The website design focused on credibility and conversion rather than visual complexity. Content structure was mapped to the client acquisition funnel. We implemented a content management system that allowed the firm to update service descriptions and case studies without technical knowledge.

The Solution

A performance-optimized corporate website featuring service descriptions, consultant profiles, case study summaries, and contact forms. Business email infrastructure with proper SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records ensuring reliable email delivery. Automated SSL certificate management for security. Content management system allowing staff to update content through a simple interface. Complete documentation for common maintenance tasks. Hosting configured for automatic backups and security updates.

The Outcome

The firm now has a professional digital presence that requires less than one hour per month of internal attention. Inbound consultation requests increased by 40% within the first quarter after launch. Email deliverability improved significantly compared to their previous personal email accounts. Website loads in under two seconds on standard connections. The firm has successfully updated service offerings and added new case studies independently using the CMS. Technical support tickets average less than one per month.

Tech Stack Used

HTML/CSS/JavaScript Strapi CMS Nginx Let's Encrypt SSL CloudFlare CDN
